industries & applications

At Weldrite Ltd we serve a wide range of industries & applications. We tailor our service to the specific needs of each industry and adapt our processes to enable us to offer an optimum solution for any market.

automotive industry

At Weldrite, we understand the needs of the automotive sector and the products we manufacture are geared around meeting its specific demands.

Weldrite’s services play a major role in supporting the storage and transportation requirements of the automotive industry – contributing to the safety, efficiency and profitability of vehicle component suppliers and manufacturers throughout the world.

Typical products include:

  • Stillages
  • Pallet Racking
  • Tyre Racking
  • Mobile Racking
  • Exhaust Racking
  • Automotive Pallets

construction industry

Weldrite is able to supply racking solutions specifically designed for the construction industry. This sector demands durable products manufactured to exacting standards.

Typical products include:

  • Stillages
  • Storage Pallets
  • Heavy Duty Steel Pallets
  • Roll Trolleys/Trucks

aerospace industry

Weldrite knows that aerospace industries need robust, reliable equipment to maintain efficiency. Weldrite supports the secure storage and transportation of the often high value and fragile items that are key to day to day operations.

Typical products include:

  • Racking
  • Stillages

Defence Industry

Weldrite can provide robust transportation and storage systems for a vast range of items that are critical to the top level performance of defence operations. We recognise the importance of delivering solutions to a specific time schedule and can offer rapid turnaround from order to delivery.

Typical products include:

  • Beams
  • Stillages
  • Post Pallets

Oil, Gas & Petrochemical Industry

Weldrite can fabricate a range of specialist products specifically geared to helping the energy industry operate smoothly and efficiently because we understand that even the smallest amount of downtime carries a high level of cost.

Typical products include:

  • Bespoke Racking Systems
  • Stillages
  • Guardrails and Handrails

FMCG Industry

Any business that deals with the storage or distribution of Fast Moving Consumer Goods needs warehousing solutions that provide maximum usage of storage capacity combined with the safe, secure and rapid movement of goods.

Typical products include:

  • Pallet Racking
  • Distribution Cages

Logistics, Distribution and Warehousing

With many years of experience in the manufacture and supply of specialist pallet-racking systems and material handling solutions Weldrite is ideally placed to meet the rigorous demands of the Logistics, Distribution and Warehousing industries.

Typical products include:

  • Strong, reliable construction for constant heavy-duty use
  • Maximum safety and accessibility
  • Flexibility to deal with individual needs
  • Bespoke requirements catered for

Marine & Shipping

Suppliers to the marine & shipping industries require storage solutions that can cope with large numbers of components, some of which will be heavy and bulky. Weldrite is able to manufacture and supply a comprehensive range of products that combine intelligent design with robust construction.

Typical products include:

  • Pallet Racking
  • Stillages

public sector

High quality construction which extends the lifetime of our products and provides high level of safety during installation and usage means that public sector industries can be confident in specifying Weldrite products.

healthcare

Every area has its own set of unique demands and we are able to match the specific needs of each department with flexible, sustainable solutions.

Typical services include the manufacture of storage and handling products for medical equipment, medicines and patient records.

education

Weldrite products can be adapted to meet the wide and varied needs of the education sector. We recognise the need to store, move and access a great many items in a limited space and our many years of fabrication experience makes us ideally placed to meet those requirements.

public sector

Weldrite’s experience and commitment to quality enables us to meet the stringent demands of the retail industry. Our tough reliable products satisfy many aspects of our customers’ requirements – safety, durability, flexibility, easy access and high-density storage.

Typical products include:

  • Tough, reliable construction
  • Comply with safety and hygiene standards
  • Flexible solution to meet your business needs
